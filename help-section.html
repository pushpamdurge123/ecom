Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or through the courier service's tracking page.

2. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return through our returns page, and we will guide you through the process.

3. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our 24/7 customer support team via phone, email, or live chat. All contact details are available in the section below.

4. Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Once the order is confirmed, the delivery address cannot be changed. However, you can cancel the order before it's shipped and place a new order with the updated address.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Cash on delivery is also available for selected regions.
